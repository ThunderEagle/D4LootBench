# Rule: IP Range 800-900  (ItemPower, Recolor)
Recolor items with item power between 800 and 900

# Rule: Legendary+ Items  (Rarity)
Show all legendary and above items

# Rule: Ancestral Items  (ItemProperties mask=4)
Show all ancestral items

# Rule: 2+ Greater Affixes  (GreaterAffix count)
Show items with at least 2 greater affixes

# Rule: Codex Upgrades  (Codex)
Show items that can upgrade a codex of power

# Rule: Charms & Seals  (ItemType)
Show all charms and horadric seals

# Rule: Required CDR + Armor, CDR must be GA  (AffixCondition with GreaterEntries)
Show items that have both cooldown reduction and armor, with GA cooldown reduction

# Rule: Optional Dex or Int  (OptionalAffixCondition)
Show items that have either dexterity or intelligence

# Rule: Specific Uniques — Fists of Fate and Penitent Greaves  (SpecificUnique)
Show Fists of Fate and Penitent Greaves

# Rule: Sword 800+ Item Power, Rare or better  (ItemPower + ItemType + Rarity)
Show rare or better swords and two-handed swords with item power 800 or higher

# Rule: Sescheron's Fury talisman set  (TalismanSet + Rarity + ItemType)
Show charms from the Sescheron's Fury talisman set
